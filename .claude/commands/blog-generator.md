Research and create a new SEO-optimized MDX blog post for Komendly (AI video testimonial generator SaaS at komendly.com). use subagents and tools in parallel. Follow this workflow:

## STEP 1 - RESEARCH & DISCOVERY

- Spawn up to 10 subagents in parallel for comprehensive research & discovery.
- Search Google for trending topics related to: "video testimonials", "customer reviews marketing", "AI avatars", "social proof", "video marketing 2025"
- Find 3-5 recent articles (published in last 3 months) in this space
- Identify gaps in competitor content that we can fill
- Look for latest statistics, studies, or data about video testimonials and conversion rates
- Check what's trending on Reddit r/marketing, r/entrepreneur, r/smallbusiness related to testimonials/reviews

## STEP 2 - TOPIC SELECTION

Based on research, choose ONE topic that is:
- Currently trending or has search volume
- Not heavily covered by competitors
- Relevant to Komendly's target audience (small businesses, real estate agents, restaurants, SaaS companies)
- Can be tied to recent news, trends, or data

## STEP 3 - CREATE THE BLOG POST

Write a 1,500-2,000 word blog post ready for our MDX blog system. Keep it conversational and to the point. something people will actually read. no slop. no fluff. avoid common AI phrases.

### FRONTMATTER FORMAT:
```yaml
---
title: "[SEO-optimized title with target keyword]"
description: "[155 character meta description]"
publishDate: "[current date in YYYY-MM-DD format]"
author: "Komendly Team"
tags: [relevant, tags, here]
keywords: "[primary keyword, secondary keyword, related terms]"
slug: "[url-friendly-slug]"
featured: false
---
```

### CONTENT STRUCTURE:
1. **Hook** (2-3 sentences addressing pain point)
2. **Introduction** (150-200 words setting up the problem)
3. **5-7 H2 sections** with actionable insights
4. Include **real statistics** from your research (cite sources)
5. Add comparison tables, bullet lists, numbered steps where relevant
6. **FAQ section** (4-5 questions with schema-ready answers)
7. Strong **conclusion** with clear CTA

### SEO REQUIREMENTS:
- Target keyword in: title, first 100 words, 2+ H2s, conclusion, URL slug
- Natural keyword density (1-2%)
- Include LSI keywords from research
- Internal links to: /pricing, /register, other relevant blog posts
- External links to authoritative sources (2-3)
- Alt text suggestions for any image placeholders

### CONVERSION ELEMENTS:
- **CTA after intro:** "Try Komendly â†’"
- **Mid-article:** Include success metric or social proof
- **End CTA:** Strong call to action with benefit statement

### WRITING STYLE:
- Conversational but professional
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Use "you" to speak directly to reader
- Include specific examples and scenarios
- Data-driven where possible
- Match Komendly's brand voice: direct, problem-solving, no fluff. avoid common ai phrases.
